The Scale of the Crisis

A high-intensity earthquake has struck the central region, leaving a trail of critical damage in vulnerable communities.

With a depth of just 10 kilometers, the seismic movement severely affected highly populated areas. Residential buildings collapsed, drinking water supply systems were disabled, and public utility networks suffered massive cuts, isolating dozens of communities at the most critical moment.  

Immediate civil mobilization is indispensable. The Unicoin Foundation matching fund and the Influur amplification network guarantee that every contribution reaches the acquisition of vital supplies for the affected population directly and transparently.
